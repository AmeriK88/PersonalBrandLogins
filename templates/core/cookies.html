1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time.

2. Types of cookies we use

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly, such as security, load balancing or remembering basic preferences. They cannot be disabled from our systems.

Analytics cookies (if enabled)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website (e.g. pages visited, time spent, clicks) in an aggregated and anonymous manner.

Third-party cookies

Some content (such as embedded videos or social media content) may set cookies from external providers (e.g. YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn).

3. Managing cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

4. Consent

By continuing to browse this website, you consent to the use of essential cookies. Analytics or non-essential cookies will only be used if you accept them.
